Our Story

Built on the Water

NorthWakeSurfCo grew out of a lifelong love for boating and watersports. We grew up on the water and saw how much it brings people together — families, friends, and first-timers all finding something special out there.

Our goal is simple: create a fun, safe, and memorable experience for everyone on board. Whether you're learning to get up for the first time or dialing in an advanced trick, we're here for every moment of it.
